Our Adoption Process

Finding your perfect canine companion is an exciting journey! Our adoption process is designed to match dogs with loving families and create lasting bonds. Learn about the steps involved, from application to home visits. When you adopt from Paws Haven Rescue, you're not just gaining a furry friend you're becoming part of a community that cares. Find out what's included in our adoption packages, including vaccinations, spaying/neutering, and more.

If you're interested in adopting one of our other pets, please fill out ourvolunteering application form. Our team will review your application and get in touch with you to schedule a meeting with your potential new pet.
